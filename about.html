About Me

My name is Ashley Arciero owner of BEAUTYPOP! My passion has always been hair and makeup and wanting to start my own line. Recently I took the step to acheive my goal and started to launch my brand. I started with the product mink lashes because they are my favorite accessory when doing my own makeup! People often ask me how I came up with the name. The word BEAUTYPOP has alot of meaning to me. I have always loved beauty, modeling, and fashion. I think everyone has a BEAUTY POP in themselves and should bring that out! My BEAUTYPOP is my colorful hair.Another reason why I choose this name is because I have a friend who passed away who would always call me lollipop. He inspired me. Lollipops are super colorful and I thought how perfect would this be for the name. RIP Paul thank you for making me step up to start this line!! Stay tuned for more products to come!!
